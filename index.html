Pew Pew

Instructions

  • Try to connect at least four pieces of the same color to have them clear off of the board
  • Side arrow keys move pieces from side to side
  • Up arrow key swaps the colors of the piece
  • Spacebar key rotates the piece
  • Down key moves piece down faster
  • Press Enter to PAUSE the game
