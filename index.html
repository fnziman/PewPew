Instructions

  • Try to connect at least four pieces to have them clear off of the board
  • Side arrow keys move pieces from side to side
  • Up arrow key swaps the colors of the piece
  • Spacebar rotates the piece
  • Use down key to move down faster
  • Press Enter to PAUSE the game
